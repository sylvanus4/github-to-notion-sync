# Goose Feature Launch Playbook

Generate a phased feature launch plan covering pre-launch (internal prep, beta, content), launch day (channels, timing, messaging), and post-launch (monitoring, iteration, case study). Designed for product and marketing teams who need a repeatable launch framework without a dedicated launch manager.

Adapted from [gooseworks-ai/goose-skills](https://github.com/gooseworks-ai/goose-skills) composites/feature-launch-playbook.

## When to Use

- "Create a feature launch plan"
- "Build a launch playbook for our new feature"
- "기능 출시 플레이북", "피처 런칭 계획"
- "What's the launch checklist for this feature?"

## Do NOT Use

- For GTM strategy with battlecards (use pm-go-to-market)
- For full release lifecycle with CI/CD (use release-commander)
- For PRD writing (use pm-execution)

## Methodology

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch (T-14 to T-1)
- **Internal readiness:** docs, sales enablement, support training
- **Beta program:** select users, feedback loops, bug triage
- **Content pipeline:** blog post, changelog, social, email
- **Positioning:** headline, subhead, key messages, proof points

### Phase 2: Launch Day (T-0)
- **Timing:** optimal day/time for your audience
- **Channel activation sequence:**
  1. Product changelog/in-app
  2. Email to existing users
  3. Blog post
  4. Social media (LinkedIn, Twitter)
  5. Community (Slack, Discord, Reddit)
  6. PR/media (if applicable)
- **Internal comms:** all-hands announcement, Slack

### Phase 3: Post-Launch (T+1 to T+30)
- **Monitoring:** adoption metrics, error rates, support tickets
- **Fast follow:** bug fixes, UX improvements from feedback
- **Content:** case study from early adopter, how-to guide
- **Retrospective:** what worked, what didn't, process improvements

## Output: Launch Playbook with timeline, channel plan, messaging, checklists, and success metrics
