# Goose ICP Website Audit

Audit your website through the lens of your ideal customer profile. Evaluates whether your site's messaging, content, proof points, and conversion paths match what your ICP actually cares about — vs what you think they care about. Identifies ICP-messaging gaps and produces a prioritized fix list.

Adapted from [gooseworks-ai/goose-skills](https://github.com/gooseworks-ai/goose-skills) composites/icp-website-audit.

## When to Use

- "Audit our website for ICP alignment"
- "Does our site speak to our target customer?"
- "ICP 웹사이트 감사", "타겟 고객 메시지 정합성"
- "Website-ICP gap analysis"

## Do NOT Use

- For full CRO audit without ICP focus (use marketing-conversion-ops)
- For ad-to-landing page consistency (use goose-ad-landing-page-auditor)
- For design/UX audit (use ux-expert)

## Methodology

### Input: ICP Definition
- Role/title, company size, industry
- Primary pain points (top 3)
- Buying triggers (what makes them start looking)
- Decision criteria (how they evaluate solutions)
- Objections (what holds them back)

### Audit Dimensions

#### 1. Homepage Message Match (0-10)
- Does the hero speak directly to the ICP's #1 pain?
- Would the ICP recognize themselves within 5 seconds?
- Is the language customer-language or company-jargon?

#### 2. Proof Point Relevance (0-10)
- Do logos/testimonials match ICP company profile?
- Are case studies from similar companies/roles?
- Are metrics relevant to ICP's KPIs?

#### 3. Feature Prioritization (0-10)
- Are features ordered by ICP importance or internal roadmap?
- Do feature descriptions explain value (not just capability)?
- Are ICP-critical features easy to find?

#### 4. Objection Handling (0-10)
- Are top ICP objections addressed on the site?
- Is there a clear comparison or "vs" content?
- Is pricing transparent (if ICP expects it)?

#### 5. Conversion Path Alignment (0-10)
- Does the primary CTA match ICP buying behavior?
- Is the form length appropriate for ICP (founder vs enterprise)?
- Is the next step clear and low-friction?
