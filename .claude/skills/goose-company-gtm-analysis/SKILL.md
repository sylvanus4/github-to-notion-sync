# Goose Company Current GTM Analysis

Reverse-engineer any company's go-to-market strategy from their public signals: website messaging, pricing, content, ads, hiring, and community. Produces a structured GTM assessment covering positioning, channels, sales motion, and growth model. Useful for competitive intelligence or evaluating a company before partnership/acquisition.

Adapted from [gooseworks-ai/goose-skills](https://github.com/gooseworks-ai/goose-skills) composites/company-current-gtm-analysis.

## When to Use

- "Analyze [company]'s go-to-market strategy"
- "Reverse-engineer how [competitor] sells"
- "회사 GTM 분석", "경쟁사 시장 진출 전략 분석"
- "What's [company]'s growth model?"

## Do NOT Use

- For designing your own GTM strategy (use pm-go-to-market)
- For pricing-only competitive tracking (use goose-competitive-pricing-intel)
- For full market sizing without GTM focus (use pm-market-research)

## Methodology

### Phase 1: Positioning & Messaging
- Homepage hero messaging: who, what, why
- Product positioning: category, differentiator, proof
- ICP signals: who their marketing speaks to
- Value proposition: primary and secondary claims

### Phase 2: Channel Mix
- **Website/SEO:** content strategy, blog topics, pSEO patterns
- **Paid:** Google/LinkedIn/Meta ad presence and messaging
- **Social:** platforms, frequency, content types, engagement
- **Community:** Slack, Discord, Reddit, forums
- **Events:** conferences, webinars, podcasts
- **Partnerships:** integrations, co-marketing, channel partners

### Phase 3: Sales Motion
- Self-serve vs sales-led vs PLG indicators
- Pricing model and tier structure
- Demo/trial flow
- Hiring patterns (SDR/AE/CS ratios from job postings)

### Phase 4: Growth Model
- Estimated growth stage (pre-PMF, scaling, mature)
- Primary growth lever (content, product virality, outbound, partnerships)
- Moat indicators (network effects, switching costs, data advantage)

## Output: Structured GTM Assessment with sections on positioning, channels, sales motion, growth model, and strategic implications
