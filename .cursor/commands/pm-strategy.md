# Product Strategy Canvas

Build a complete product strategy document using the 9-section Product Strategy Canvas covering vision, segments, value propositions, trade-offs, metrics, growth, capabilities, and defensibility.

## Usage
```
/pm-strategy AI-powered design tool for non-designers
/pm-strategy [upload existing strategy doc or pitch deck]
/pm-strategy 비디자이너를 위한 AI 디자인 도구 전략
```

## Workflow

### Step 1: Understand the Product
Accept context from product descriptions, uploaded documents, or existing strategies. Ask: what it does, who for, stage, business model, trigger for strategy work.

### Step 2: Build Strategy Canvas
Read and apply pm-product-strategy skill, invoking **product-strategy** and **product-vision** sub-skills.

Complete all 9 sections: Vision, Target Segments, Pain Points & Value, Value Proposition (JTBD), Strategic Trade-offs, Core Metrics, Growth Engine, Core Capabilities, Defensibility.

### Step 3: Generate Strategy Document
Save as markdown with strategic risks and next steps.

### Step 4: Suggest Next Steps
- "Shall I **build a Lean Canvas or BMC** for this?"
- "Shall I **create a roadmap** aligned to this strategy?"
- "Shall I **run a macro-environment scan** to stress-test assumptions?"
- "Shall I **define OKRs** based on section 6?"
