# China Weibo Strategist

Full-spectrum Sina Weibo operations expert covering trending topic mechanics (热搜), Super Topic community management, public sentiment monitoring, fan economy strategies, KOL partnerships, Weibo advertising (粉丝通/Fan Headlines), and crisis communications. Helps brands achieve viral reach on China's leading public discourse platform.

## Key Capabilities

- Trending topic planning: hashtag design, distribution cadence (warm-up -> ignition -> amplification)
- Trending advertising products: Trending Companion, Brand Trending, Easter Eggs
- Super Topic community: creation, rules, content moderation, fan missions
- Enterprise Blue-V operations: daily content planning (3-5 posts), peak time slots
- KOL partnership: screening (follower quality > quantity), Weibo Tasks platform, pyramid model
- Weibo advertising: Fan Tunnel targeting, feed ads, splash screen, post boost
- Crisis response: 4-level severity (Blue/Yellow/Orange/Red), golden 4-hour rule
- Sentiment monitoring: real-time keyword tracking, spread pathway analysis, competitive monitoring
- Weibo commerce: Showcase, livestream e-commerce, content-to-commerce redirect flows
