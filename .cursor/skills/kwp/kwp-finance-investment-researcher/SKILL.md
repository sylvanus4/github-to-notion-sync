# KWP Finance Investment Researcher

Institutional-quality investment research: fundamental analysis, quantitative screening, due diligence, and portfolio analytics. Produces research reports with bull/bear cases, catalyst timelines, thesis breakers, and valuation frameworks across public equities, private markets, and alternative assets.

Adapted from [msitarzewski/agency-agents](https://github.com/msitarzewski/agency-agents) finance/finance-investment-researcher.

## When to Use

- "Investment research report on [company]", "equity research"
- "Due diligence checklist", "DD on [company]"
- "Competitive moat assessment", "Porter's Five Forces for [industry]"
- "Factor screening", "quantitative stock screen"
- "투자 리서치", "기업 실사", "경쟁 우위 분석", "밸류에이션"
- "Bull/bear case analysis", "thesis breaker identification"
- "Alternative data analysis for investment"

## Do NOT Use

- For daily stock trading signals (use daily-stock-check)
- For personal portfolio position sizing (use trading-position-sizer)
- For market environment analysis (use trading-market-environment-analysis)
- For financial modeling without investment thesis (use kwp-finance-analyst)
- For AlphaEar sentiment analysis (use alphaear-sentiment)
- For backtesting strategies (use trading-backtest-expert)

## Capabilities

### Fundamental Analysis
- Financial statement quality assessment (revenue, earnings sustainability, cash conversion)
- Competitive moat scoring (switching costs, network effects, scale advantages, brand)
- Management quality evaluation (capital allocation track record, insider activity, governance)
- ESG integration with material factor identification

### Quantitative Analysis
- Multi-factor screens (value, quality, momentum, growth)
- Risk metrics (beta, VaR, Sharpe, Sortino, max drawdown)
- Portfolio attribution and risk decomposition
- Statistical regression and factor decomposition

### Due Diligence
- Revenue verification and customer concentration analysis
- Technology and IP assessment
- Operational DD (supply chain, customer references, patent review)
- Red flag identification (related-party transactions, accounting anomalies)

### Research Output
- Full research reports: thesis, catalysts, bear case, valuation, thesis breakers
- Due diligence checklists (financial, operational, market, legal dimensions)
- Comparable and DCF valuation with weighted probability targets

## Methodology

1. **Screening**: Run quantitative screens; monitor industry themes and structural shifts
2. **Initial Assessment**: Review 3 years of financials; map competitive landscape; rough valuation range
3. **Deep Dive**: Detailed financial model; primary research; stress-test against bear scenarios
4. **Thesis Formulation**: Actionable recommendation with conviction level, sizing guidance, and monitoring triggers
5. **Monitoring**: Track thesis breakers and catalyst timelines; update on material developments

## Output

Investment research reports with executive summary, quantified bull/bear cases, catalyst timelines, valuation tables (DCF + comps), thesis breaker triggers, and risk-adjusted return estimates.
