# Market News Analysis Report Template

Full report structure. Generate reports ranked by Impact Score (highest first).

## Report Structure

```markdown
# Market News Analysis Report - [Date Range]

## Executive Summary
[3-4 sentences: period, # events, dominant theme, top 1-2 impacts]

## Market Impact Rankings
| Rank | Event | Date | Impact Score | Asset Classes | Market Reaction |

## Detailed Event Analysis
[For each event, provide:]
### [Rank]. [Event Name] (Impact Score: X)
- Event Date, Type, Source
- Event Summary (3-4 sentences)
- Market Reaction: Immediate (equities, bonds, commodities, currencies, VIX) and Follow-Through
- Pattern Comparison: Expected vs Actual (Consistent/Amplified/Dampened/Inverse)
- Impact Assessment: Price impact, Breadth, Forward significance, Calculated score
- Sector-Specific Impacts (if relevant)
- Geopolitical-Commodity Correlation (if geopolitical event)

## Thematic Synthesis
- Dominant Market Narrative
- Interconnected Events
- Market Regime Assessment (Risk-On/Risk-Off, Sector Rotation)
- Anomalies and Surprises

## Commodity Market Deep Dive
- Energy, Precious Metals, Base Metals, Agricultural (if relevant)

## Forward-Looking Implications
- Market Positioning Insights
- Upcoming Catalysts
- Risk Scenarios (3-5)

## Data Sources and Methodology
- News Sources, Analysis Period, Market Data, Knowledge Base References
```

**File naming:** `outputs/reports/trading/market_news_analysis_[START]_to_[END].md`

**Quality:** Objective, quantified (%), cited sources, causation discipline, English throughout.
