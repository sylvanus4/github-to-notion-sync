# Paper Review Template (Korean)

Use this template to generate the structured paper review markdown. Fill every
section with specific content from the paper. Do not leave sections empty — if
information is unavailable, state that explicitly.

Write in formal Korean academic tone (학술적 한국어). Reference specific
sections, figures, tables, and equations from the paper.

---

## Writing Guidelines

1. **Specificity over generality**: "성능이 좋다" 대신 "SQuAD 벤치마크에서 기존 SOTA 대비 F1 3.2% 향상"
2. **Evidence-backed claims**: Every strength/weakness must cite specific sections, figures, or tables
3. **Balanced assessment**: Avoid purely positive or purely negative reviews
4. **Actionable insights**: Weaknesses should suggest improvement directions
5. **Consistent terminology**: Use the paper's original terms alongside Korean translations
6. **Quantitative where possible**: Include specific numbers, percentages, ratios from the paper
