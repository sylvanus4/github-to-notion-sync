# Bubble Detector Action Playbook (v2.1)

## Risk Budgets by Phase

| Phase | Score | Risk Budget | ATR Stop | New Positions | Short-Selling |
|-------|-------|-------------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| Normal | 0-4 | 100% | 2.0x | Normal | Not allowed |
| Caution | 5-7 | 70-80% | 1.8x | -50% sizing | Not recommended |
| Elevated Risk | 8-9 | 50-70% | 1.6x | Quality only | Cautious (2/7 conditions) |
| Euphoria | 10-12 | 40-50% | 1.5x | Major pullbacks only | Active (3/7 conditions) |
| Critical | 13-15 | 20-30% | 1.2x | None | Recommended (5/7 conditions) |

## Detailed Actions

### Normal (0-4 points)
- Continue normal investment strategy
- Set ATR 2.0x trailing stop
- Apply stair-step profit-taking rule (+20% take 25%)

### Caution (5-7 points)
- Begin partial profit-taking (20-30% reduction)
- Tighten ATR to 1.8x
- Reduce new position sizing by 50%
- Wait for clearer reversal signals before shorting

### Elevated Risk (8-9 points) -- NEW in v2.1
- Increase profit-taking (30-50% reduction)
- Tighten ATR to 1.6x
- New positions: highly selective, quality only
- Begin building cash reserves
- Short only after 2/7 composite conditions, small exploratory positions (10-15% size)

### Euphoria (10-12 points)
- Accelerate stair-step profit-taking (50-60% reduction)
- Tighten ATR to 1.5x
- No new long positions except on major pullbacks
- Short after 3/7 conditions, small positions (20-25%), defined risk only

### Critical (13-15 points)
- Major profit-taking or full hedge
- ATR 1.2x or fixed stop-loss
- Cash preservation mode
- Short after 5/7 conditions, scale in small, consider put options

## Composite Conditions for Short-Selling (7 Items)

Confirm at least 3 before shorting:
1. Weekly chart shows lower highs
2. Volume peaks out
3. Leverage indicators drop sharply (margin debt decline)
4. Media/search trends peak out
5. Weak stocks start to break down first
6. VIX surges (spike above 20)
7. Fed/policy shift signals
